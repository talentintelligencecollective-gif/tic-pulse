## Full Description (4000 chars max)

TIC Pulse is the daily companion for talent intelligence professionals. Built by the Talent Intelligence Collective (TIC), it brings together curated news, expert videos, and industry podcasts into a single, clean feed — so you never miss what matters in the world of work.

WHAT YOU GET

A global news feed pulling from 50+ search topics across 9 geographies. Every article is summarised by AI into a quick TL;DR with category tags, so you can scan the headlines in seconds and dive deeper when something catches your eye.

A video hub with content from leading TI and HR channels — Josh Bersin, AIHR, Recruiting Brainfood, and more. Filter by type (podcasts, panels, events, shorts) or search by topic.

A podcast player with episodes from the TIC Podcast, Recruiting Future, Chad & Cheese, People Analytics World, and other essential shows. Play episodes directly in the app with full playback controls.

A newsletter builder to curate your favourite articles into a branded email briefing. Pick a theme, add your intro, preview the layout, and export as HTML — ready to paste into your email tool.

WHO IT'S FOR

Talent intelligence analysts, people analytics professionals, HR leaders, workforce strategists, recruiters, and anyone who needs to stay current on how AI, automation, skills, compensation, and labour markets are reshaping the world of work.

FEATURES

• Real-time global news from the Financial Times, Bloomberg, Reuters, The Guardian, Forbes, SHRM, People Management, and hundreds more
• 8 topic categories: Talent Strategy, Labour Market, Automation, Executive Moves, Compensation, Workforce Planning, Skills, DEI
• Regional filtering: North America, Europe, Asia Pacific, Middle East, Africa
• Personalised "For You" feed that learns from your reading habits
• Trending tags updated every 48 hours from live article data
• Video hub with embedded YouTube player
• Podcast player with real audio playback
• Newsletter builder with 6 themes and custom brand colours
• Bookmark and like articles, synced across devices
• Comments on articles — discuss with the TIC community
• Share to LinkedIn, WhatsApp, Email, X with one tap

ABOUT TIC

The Talent Intelligence Collective is a global community of talent intelligence practitioners founded by Toby Culshaw. TIC connects TI professionals through events, content, and knowledge sharing to advance the practice of talent intelligence worldwide.

Free to use. No ads. No paywalls.
