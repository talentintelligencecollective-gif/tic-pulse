Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

TIC Pulse ("the App") is operated by the Talent Intelligence Collective ("TIC", "we", "us"). This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

What we collect

When you create an account, we collect your email address, name, and optionally your company and job title. This information is used to authenticate you and personalise your experience.

When you use the App, we record your interactions (likes, bookmarks, shares, comments) to power features like your personalised feed and saved articles. We also store the text of any comments you post.

How we use your data

Your data is used to: provide and improve the App's features, personalise your news feed based on your reading preferences, display your name on comments you post, and sync your likes and bookmarks across devices.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not display advertising. We do not share your data with advertisers or data brokers.

Third-party services

The App uses the following third-party services:

Supabase — database and authentication. Your account data and app content are stored on Supabase's infrastructure. Supabase Privacy Policy.

Netlify — hosting and serverless functions. Netlify Privacy Policy.

Google News RSS — news article discovery. No personal data is sent to Google through this integration.

YouTube — embedded video playback. YouTube's own privacy policy applies when you watch embedded videos. Google Privacy Policy.

Data storage and security

Your data is stored in Supabase (hosted on AWS infrastructure). All connections use HTTPS encryption. Database access is protected by Row Level Security policies ensuring users can only access their own engagement data.

Your rights

You can delete your account and associated data at any time by contacting us. You can delete individual comments from within the App. Under GDPR (if applicable), you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data.

Cookies

The App uses essential session cookies for authentication. We do not use tracking cookies, analytics cookies, or advertising cookies.

Children

TIC Pulse is designed for professionals and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through the App.

Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact the Talent Intelligence Collective at hello@talentintelligencecollective.com.
